### Q6. Bit Operations - 5

**Problem Description**

What are the values of \( a \& a \), \( a | a \), \( a \oplus a \)?

**Answer Options**

- [ ] \( a \& a = a \), \( a | a = a \), \( a \oplus a = a \)
- [ ] \( a \& a = a \), \( a | a = a \), \( a \oplus a = 0 \)
- [ ] \( a \& a = 0 \), \( a | a = a \), \( a \oplus a = a \)
- [ ] \( a \& a = 0 \), \( a | a = a \), \( a \oplus a = 0 \)
